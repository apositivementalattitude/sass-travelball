Who I Am

As the Director of California Dream, I’m proud to lead a program that focuses on more than just basketball. Our mission is to develop well-rounded athletes who excel not only in their sport, but also in their character and leadership. From our youngest players just starting their basketball journey to our high school athletes preparing for the next level, we’re dedicated to building skills, confidence, and a passion for the game.

Our coaching staff brings years of experience, and we believe in creating an environment where every player is challenged, supported, and given the tools to succeed. We’re not just focused on winning games—we’re focused on helping each athlete reach their highest potential, both on and off the court. I’m excited to continue working with our talented players and families, and I look forward to seeing their growth as they progress through our program.

The Team

Our travel basketball program is dedicated to providing young athletes, from ages 8 to 18, with the opportunity to grow as both players and individuals. We emphasize skill development, teamwork, and a strong work ethic, ensuring our athletes are prepared for the challenges of competitive basketball at every level. Starting with our youth teams, we build foundational skills and a love for the game, progressing through middle school to high school where we refine those talents and prepare athletes for the next phase of their basketball journey.

With a commitment to excellence, our experienced coaching staff focuses on individualized development, fostering leadership, discipline, and perseverance. Through rigorous training, competitive tournament play, and a supportive environment, we help our players reach their full potential. Whether our athletes aim to play at the high school, collegiate, or professional level, our program equips them with the skills, knowledge, and character to succeed both on and off the court.
